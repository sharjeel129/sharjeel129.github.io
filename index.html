I am an Undergraduate Student in Physics (Mathematics Minor) at Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS), where I work with Prof. Adam Zaman on Open Quantum Dynamics and Information Theory.

My broad research interests include Quantum Information Theory and Quantum Many-Body Systems – in particular, I am interested in Quantum Error Correction through Decoherence reduction and Lindblad Master Equations associated with Markovian Dynamics.

I also work at the Lahore Grammar School Defence Campus as a full time public speaking coach. My non-academic interests include tennis, mixed martial arts and video games.
