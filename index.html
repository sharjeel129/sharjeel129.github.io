Sharjeel Ahmad

Masters in Physics · UIUC

I am a Graduate Student in Physics at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where I am working with Prof. Eric Chitambar on Optimizing Quantum Teleportation Protocols.

I have previously worked with Prof. Paul Kwiat on multi-depth holography and linear optics as well as with Prof. Kejie Fang on Quantum Non-Gaussian Correlations, Photon Wavelength Conversion and Higher Dimensional Quantum Teleportation Protocols.

My broad research interests include Quantum Information Science and Quantum Optics – in particular, I am interested in Quantum Error Correction and building Fault Tolerant Protocols using various analytical and computational tools.

My non-academic interests include tennis, parliamentary debating and futures trading.
